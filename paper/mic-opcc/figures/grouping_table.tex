\caption{
Ablation study of different autoregressive grouping strategies using the MVUB dataset~\citep{MVUB}.
\textbf{Without Grouping}: No voxel grouping is applied and decoding follows a fully parallel scheme. All voxel $v$ of all octree-nodes $\mathbf{o}_t$ are decoded a in single stage.
\textbf{Sequential Grouping}: One voxel $v_{g}$ of all octree-nodes $\mathbf{o}_{t}$ are decoded in parallel at each monotonic step $g \in [1\dots8]$.
\textbf{Progressive Grouping}: The proposed strategy, which skips a subset of octree-nodes during early stages ($g \leq 4$) to reduce uncertainty, and progressively decodes multiple voxel candidates per node in later stages ($g > 6$) as contextual information becomes richer.
\textit{* Peak memory spikes observed during backpropagation.}
